# Business Sales Performance Analytics

> **Task 1 | Data Analytics Internship/Training Project**

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to understand sales performance, identify important trends, and support data-driven business decision-making.

The analysis explores revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value customers, and regional performance. The insights were presented through an interactive Power BI dashboard to make business performance easier to understand.

This project was completed as **Task 1** as part of a Data Analytics internship/training program.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze business sales data to understand overall performance
- Identify important sales patterns and trends
- Find top-performing products and high-value customers
- Compare sales performance across different countries and regions
- Create an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Generate meaningful business insights and recommendations

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Revenue Trends
Analyzed how sales and revenue changed over time using year-wise and month-wise trends to understand overall business performance.

### 🛍️ Top-Selling Products
Identified products contributing the highest revenue and compared their sales performance.

### 👥 High-Value Customers
Analyzed customer data to identify customers generating the highest business value.

### 🌍 Regional Performance
Compared sales performance across different countries and regions to identify strong and weak markets.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

The interactive Power BI dashboard includes:

- **Total Sales** – KPI Card
- **Sales Trends** – Line Chart
- **Product Performance** – Bar Chart
- **Country/Region Performance** – Pie Chart
- **Top Customers** – Table Visualization

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

1. Imported the dataset into Power BI

3. Created required DAX measures and KPIs

5. Designed interactive dashboard visualizations
6. Generated business insights from the analysis
7. Provided data-driven recommendations
8. Performed final dashboard formatting and reporting

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Identified overall revenue and sales performance trends
- Identified products contributing significantly to total sales
- Analyzed high-value customers based on business contribution
- Compared performance across different countries and regions
- Used data visualization to identify patterns that can support business decisions

## 📌 Outcome

This project demonstrates how raw business sales data can be transformed into meaningful insights using data analysis and visualization.

The Power BI dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance and helps stakeholders quickly understand sales trends, customer value, product performance, and regional differences for better decision-making.
